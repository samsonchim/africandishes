Chapati

"Sukuma Wiki is a Swahili phrase meaning push the week. What is really being pushed is the family food budget to stretch a bit longer. Sukuma wiki is a mixture of kale, meat, spices and at times spinach to make a savory dish. It can also be prepared without meat and be just as tasty. In such a case, it is flavored with beef bouillon cubes. It is a very popular meal among Kenyan families because of its simplicity and affordability. Sukuma Wiki is commonly served alongside Ugali. "

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of both kale and spinach
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 large onion

Preparation

  1. Remove the stalks from the vegetable.

  2. Shred them into your desired size using a sharp knife.

  3. In a saucepan, heat oil then brown the onions.

  4. Chop the tomatoes and add them into the light brown onions.

  5. Add salt and crushed the bouillon cubes and cook for 1 min.

  6. Add the cut vegetables and cook for 3 mins.

  7. Remove from fire and serve

DANBU NAMA

Ever had meat floss? Dambu nama looks like meat that has been through a thorough beating. The flavourful meat-snack is made of fried shredded meat with suya spices. Traditionally, the meat is pounded in a large mortar, but the modern kitchen can use a food processor to roughly blend the boiled meat to shreds. You can take it to the extreme and have a bowl of dambu nama with you instead of popcorn when watching a movie. A variety of recipes can be found on Nigerian food blog

Awara Dairy

CONTRARY TO POPULAR BELEIFS NIGERIAN DIARY 'AWARA' IS NOT CHEESE. IT IS MADE BY ADDING A COAGULENT TO FRESH MILK AND EATEN WITH MEALS OR AS A SNACK.

KITCHEN TOOLS YOU NEED

  • Cloth sieve
  • Strainer or colander
  • Knives
  • Large pot, Frying pan

INGREDIENTS

  • Cow milk
  • Sour milk or lime juice
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Sieve the cow milk and pour into a big pot.

  2. Boil the milk on medium heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour; closely watching and stirring frequently so it doesn't burn or boil over

  3. Reduce the heat, add in your coagulant of choiceâ€”lime juice or sour milk. (then do not stir), the tofu should start to form

  4. Pack the curds in a sieve or colander and allow excess water to strain out.

  5. Let it cool

  6. Cut into desired shapes and deep fry in hot oil

  7. Serve as a snack or put in soups. Enjoy!

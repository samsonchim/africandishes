PEPPERED PONMO

Ponmo is cow skin that is processed for consumption. Nigerians make amazing foods from the most amazing sources, and eating ponmo in flavorful soups makes you forget its origin. Ponmo starts on a butchery table in the local abattoir before it is roasted and washed thoroughly to take off the burnt fur. A common staple in many Nigerian soups, ponmo has no taste and is easily adaptable to absorb the flavours of meals it is submerged in

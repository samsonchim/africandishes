Fried RICE RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups parboiled rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables: (carrots, sweet peas, sweet corn and green beans)
  • 1 cup onions finely chopped
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 stock cube
  • 3 tsp oil
  • 1 cup beef liver, cut into cubes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Place parboiled rice into a pot, add stock and cook for 10- 15mins.

  2. Pre-heat the oil in a pan, throw in the onions and fry for a minute or two.

  3. Add spring onions, mixed vegetables and seasonings (thyme, curry powder, salt and stock cube).

  4. Add in rice and stir fry for about 3 to 5 minutes.

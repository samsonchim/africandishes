OJOJO (YAM FRITTERS)

Ingredients

  • 1 medium tuber of yam
  • 8 pieces okra (can be substituted with shrimps, onions, crushed fish)
  • 4 tbsp palm oil
  • Seasoning cube
  • 1/4 tsp pepper by preference
  • 1/4 tsp salt or by preference
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Peel and rinse yam

  2. Cut into slices to make grating easier.

  3. Grate yam to tiny shreds using the smallest side of the grater so that it creates a paste

  4. Rinse and cut off the top of the okra. Also grate

  5. If using a substitute to okra, grate as well so the tiny pieces blend naturally with the yam paste.

  6. Mix the yam paste, okra, and palm oil.

  7. Add seasoning, salt, and pepper.

  8. Heat up the vegetable oil to fry.

  9. You can either roll into balls, or scoop with spoon to fry. Do not overcrowd the pan

  10. Fry the batches for about 10 minutes. Enjoy!

TOMATO STEW

Starter-Sauce

  • 2 Red Bell Peppers
  • 1 habanero
  • 1 Chili pepper
  • 4 Big plum tomatoes
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  1. Blend the peppers, tomatoes and onions
  2. Add oil in the Pot
  3. Pour the blended sauce (You don't need to heat the oil). Note that if you heat the oil the sauce will start to fry and we really don't want to fry it in the oil we just want to boil.
  4. Boil the blended paste on a low heat between 1 hour and 1 hour 30 minutes depending on the consistency you want.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups starter sauce
  • 1/4 cup palm oil
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 Â½ cup stock (any choice of stock will work)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 stock cube
  • Salt to taste
  • Beef (or use what suits you like fish, chicken, tofu vegetables etc.)

Instructions

  1. Add the palm oil and vegetable oil to a pot.

  2. Once the oil is hot, add the starter sauce and the stock and let it come to a boil.

  3. Add paprika, curry powder and stock cube and salt to taste

  4. Add meat, fish or whatever you like

  5. Allow it to come to a boil once more.

  6. Serve with rice, beans, yam, spaghetti...... ENJOY!

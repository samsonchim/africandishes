SUYA

Suya often called 'tsire' is spicy meat which is a popular food item across Nigeria. This is kebab on a whole new level. Suya is meat fillets wrapped in blended peanuts (kuli kuli) and dried mixed pepper spices (yaji) which is sold on skewers by street vendors. The meat is usually beef, ram, or chicken.

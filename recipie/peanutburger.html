PEANUT BURGER (COATED PEANUTS)

KITCHEN TOOLS YOU NEED

  • Large bow
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Frying pot
  • Paper towels

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup roasted peanut
  • 3 cups flour
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl.

  2. Add sugar, nutmeg, baking powder, and saltr

  3. Pour your peanuts into the mixing bowl and pour a little of the whisked eggs.

  4. Combine the peanuts with the egg mix by shaking up the bowl, so that the eggs coats the peanuts.

  5. Sprinkle some plain flour and shake the bowl again so the flour coats the peanuts without any sticking together. Add more sprinkles of flour if need be

  6. Pour a little of the egg mix and shake the bowl so that the eggs coat the peanuts.

  7. Repeat step 4 and then step 5 & 4 until the egg mix is finished.

  8. Heat up the vegetable oil to deep fry the peanuts.

  9. Fry until golden brown. Takeout and place on paper towels to absorb the oil.

  10. Allow it to cool down and enjoy as a snack.

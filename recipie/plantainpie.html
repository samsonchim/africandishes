PLANTAIN PIE

KITCHEN TOOLS YOU NEED

  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Potato masher
  • Blender
  • Baking pan

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 over-ripe plantains
  • 3 eggs
  • 1⁄2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1⁄3 - 1⁄2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1⁄4 tsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tsps of vanilla extract
  • 1⁄4 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄4 tsp of ground nutmeg
  • 1⁄4 tsp of ground cloves

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.

  2. Mash plantain in a bowl or blend in a blender till purée.

  3. Pour in all the ingredients and thoroughly mix in a bowl.

  4. Rub butter onto a pie dish/loaf pan/muffin pan and apply a little flour.

  5. Pour the mixture in the pan and bake for about 45 minutes between 150°C - 180°C

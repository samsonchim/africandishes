YAM BALLS

Ingredients

  • 1 small or medium yam tuber
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tin sardine
  • Seasoning cubes
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 tbsp margarine or salted butter
  • Breadcrumbs or flour for coating
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Dried parsley
  • Garlic powder
  • Spring onions
  • Chilli flakes or spices of preference

Instructions

  1. Slice and boil the yam until soft and well cooked.

  2. While boiling, chop the pepper and onions into tiny pieces

  3. Separate the egg yolk from the egg white.

  4. Mash the boiled yam with a potato masher or fork.

  5. In one bowl, whisk onions, sardine, seasoning cubes, pepper, parsley, garlic powder, chilli flakes, egg yolk and other preferred spices. Mix together with the mashed yam

  6. Add salt to taste.

  7. In a plate or third bowl, pour in the flour or breadcrumbs.

  8. Using washed clean hands, mold the yam mixture into balls. Dip in the egg white, then roll it in the breadcrumbs. At this point you can heat up your vegetable oil or wait till youâ€™re done molding.

  9. Drop gently in the heated oil. Avoid overcrowding the oil so the temperature stays hot enough. Fry till golden.

  10. Eat and enjoy with a sauce, dip or stew!

KITCHEN TOOLS YOU NEED

Masher, whisk, pots, pan, fork, two mixing bowls, knives, chopping board

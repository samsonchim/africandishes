Motoho

"Motoho is a traditional South African dish known for its sour flavor and thick consistency. It serves as a versatile side dish that complements various meals."

Ingredients

  • Maize or sorghum meal
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix maize or sorghum meal with water to form a thick batter.
  2. Allow the mixture to ferment for a period until it develops a sour taste.
  3. Cook the fermented mixture on the stove until it reaches a thick, porridge-like consistency.
  4. Season with salt if desired.
Tips:
  • Experiment with the fermentation time to achieve the desired level of sourness.
  • Serve Motoho as a side dish with meat, vegetables, or sauces.

Gatsby

"The Gatsby is a classic South African sandwich known for its hearty and flavorful fillings. This versatile sandwich allows for a variety of ingredients, making it a popular choice for a satisfying meal on the go."

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or long roll
  • 300g steak strips (or your choice of protein)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chips (French fries)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce (ketchup)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the steak strips until browned and cooked to your liking. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté the thinly sliced onion and bell peppers until they are soft and slightly caramelized.
  3. Prepare the baguette or long roll by slicing it lengthwise without cutting it all the way through.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on one side of the bread and tomato sauce on the other.
  5. Layer the cooked steak strips, sautéed vegetables, sliced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, and chips inside the baguette.
  6. Press the sandwich down gently to compact the ingredients.
  7. Cut the Gatsby into smaller portions if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Gatsby!
Tips:
  • Feel free to customize your Gatsby with additional toppings like cheese, pickles, or hot sauce.
  • You can use different proteins such as chicken, lamb, or even vegetarian options.
  • For an authentic touch, include the classic "slap chips" (thick-cut fries) in your Gatsby.

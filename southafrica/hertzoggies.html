Hertzoggies

"Hertzoggies are delightful South African pastries filled with a delicious apricot jam mixture. These sweet treats, named after J.B.M. Hertzog, have a coconut-infused crust that perfectly complements the fruity filling. Enjoy these classic pastries with a cup of tea or coffee."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup smooth apricot jam
  • Extra desiccated coconut for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a muffin tin.
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, desiccated coconut, baking powder, and salt. Mix well and set aside.
  3. In another bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg to the butter and sugar mixture, beating until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  6. Take portions of the dough and press them into the muffin tin to form tart shells. Ensure the bottom and sides are evenly covered.
  7. Spoon a dollop of apricot jam into each tart shell.
  8. Sprinkle extra desiccated coconut over the jam in each tart.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  10. Allow the Hertzoggies to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Tips:
  • For added flavor, you can mix a bit of finely chopped preserved ginger into the apricot jam.
  • Make sure the tart shells are thick enough to hold the jam without becoming too delicate.
  • Sprinkle a little extra coconut on top for a finishing touch before baking.

Inkukhu yesintu

"Inkukhu yesintu, or Traditional Chicken, is a comforting and flavorful South African dish that celebrates home-style cooking. This recipe infuses the chicken with aromatic spices and traditional flavors, creating a delicious and hearty meal for the family."

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© chopped onions until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for an additional minute.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes and cook until they start to break down.
  5. Add chicken pieces to the pot, turning to coat them in the flavorful mixture.
  6. Season the chicken with ground coriander, ground cumin, paprika, ground turmeric, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Cover the pot and let the chicken simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  9. Serve the Inkukhu yesintu hot with your choice of side dishes.
Tips:
  • Marinate the chicken in the spice mixture for a few hours or overnight for enhanced flavor.
  • Adjust the level of spiciness by adding or reducing the amount of chili powder.
  • Serve with rice, pap, or your favorite side dishes.

Grilled Tilapia

Ingredients

  • 2 tilapia or mackerel
  • 2 oz fresh ginger
  • 1 large jalapeño pepper
  • 1 large onions.
  • 4 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 3 peices of garlic
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cut fish into two or three pieces. Clean and place them in a bowl.

  2. Blend jalapeño pepper, onions, garlic, ginger and olive oil. Pour on top of fish. Let marinate for 15 - 20 minutes.

  3. Pre-heat oven to 380 degrees.

  4. Broil on low for 8 minutes on each side.

  5. OR grill fish 5 minutes per side.

  6. Garnish with fresh red pepper, yellow pepper and onions. Serve with banku, kenkey or fried yams. Or serve alone with vegetables.

  • Total fat: 7 g (saturated 1)

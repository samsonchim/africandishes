Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 lb chicken breast (or 1.5 lb dried herring)
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 jalapeño peppers
  • 1/2 tin tomato paste
  • 2 tsp fresh parsley
  • 2 tsp fresh basil
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 box low salt beef broth
  • 1/2 lb fresh green beans

Instructions

  1. Brown meat, drain the stock and set both aside for later use.

  2. If you are using fresh jalapeño pepper, then blend the pepper, fresh tomatoes and onions together and pour into the saucepan.

  3. Or Place sliced or diced onions and pepper in a saucepan and fry for about 5 minute.

  4. Add sliced or diced tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, chopped garlic, bay leave, salt and any desired spices.

  5. Allow cooking for about 10 to 15 minutes when the sauce turns a bit dark and oil is red.

  6. Add some of the sauce meat or the dried herrings.

  7. Add in the broth and let it boil rapidly and add the rice.

  8. Lower the heat and let cook for about 10 minutes.

  9. Then add green beans and stir or fluff with a fork.

  10. Leave to steam for about 5 minutes.

  11. Remove from heat and serve with the sauté meat on the side.

Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 lb stewing beef
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 medium onions, sliced
  • 4 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tsp fresh or dried parsley
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2.5 tsp red pepper

Instructions

  1. Saute meat with sliced onions, chopped garlic and salt in medium cooking pan for 10 minutes.

  2. . Remove and place meat in baking pan at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until tender.

  3. In same medium cooking pan that held the meat, place onions with olive oil and cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Blend tomatoes and pepper. Place in medium saucepan with parsley.

  5. Remove baked meat with sauces and add to tomato sauce.

  6. Let meat sauce simmer on low heat for 10 minutes

  7. Simmer for 4 minutes and turn off heat, allowing to cool.

  8. Serve with wake, boiled brown rise, ampesie

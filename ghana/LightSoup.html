Light Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef
  • 1 whole fish (such as tilapia)
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 2 medium crabs
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tin tomato paste
  • 1 cup of olive oil
  • 1 large jalape√±o pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut meat into stewing size.

  2. Place meat, salt, tomato paste, and chopped onion in pan over medium heat.

  3. Cover and steam for about 6 minutes.

  4. In another pan, cook onions, fresh pepper and tomatoes until tender

  5. Remove onion mixture and puree.

  6. Add 3 cups of water and bring to a boil for 15 minutes

  7. Remove onions, fresh pepper, and tomatoes. Blend to puree.

  8. Pour puree sauce into soup. Add meat to broth

  9. Clean and cut fish into two peices

  10. Add mushrooms and/or crabs (optional) into the soup

  11. Boil for 15 minutes until ready

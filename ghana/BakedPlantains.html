Baked Plantains

Ingredients

  • 6 Ripe Plantains

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 380 degrees.

  2. Peel and cut plantains into 4 slices.

  3. Place them on a baking rack.

  4. Bake in oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown.

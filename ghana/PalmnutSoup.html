Palmnut Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 can palmnut
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 whole fish (i.e. tilapia)
  • 3 blue crabs or 1 rock crab
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 1/2 tin tomato paste
  • 1 medium jalape√±o

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut meat into stewing size.

  2. Add meat, tomato paste, one chopped onion and salt (optional) to medium saucepan over medium heat for 8-10 minutes until onions are soft./li>
  3. Cook tomatoes, peppers and onions in separate pan for about 10 minutes.

  4. . Clean mushrooms, fish and crabs. Break fish into two pieces and cut crabs into two. Add to simmering meat.

  5. Blend tomatoes and onions. Pour mixture on the meat with 1/3 pint of water. Bring to boil

  6. Add palmnut to mixture. Add 1 can of water

  7. Stir soup until it's smooth

  8. Cook for 25 minutes or when red oil is secreted on top of soup. Reduce to low heat for 5 minutes

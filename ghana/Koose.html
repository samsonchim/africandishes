Koose

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup wheat flour
  • 3/4 bag black-eyed bean
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. of pepper
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp. of salt

Instructions

  1. Soak black-eyed beans in water overnight. Wash and drain the beans under running water

  2. Preheat oven to 370 degrees.

  3. Remove loose skins and pour in a food processor, with the pepper, onions and salt.

  4. Add pepper, ginger, onions and salt. Blend until coarsely smooth.

  5. Correct seasoning. Mix in the wheat flour and baking powder to bind the mixture. Add pepper, ginger, onions and salt

  6. Pour mixture intogreased baking pan

  7. Remove and place on the cooling rack

  8. Cut into peices and serve

Salmon Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs salmon
  • 2 oz fresh ginger
  • 1 large jalape√±o pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut fish into about 6 pieces.

  2. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside./li>
  3. Cook tomatoes, peppers and onions in separate pan for about 10 minutes.

  4. Cook tomatoes, pepper, ginger and onions in a pan for about 10 minutes.

  5. Remove mixture and blend into puree.

  6. . Pour puree sauce in a saucepan, with about 1/3 pint of water.

  7. Cook for about 20 minutes.

  8. Add seasoned salmon

  9. Cook on medium for about 15 minutes

  10. Turn off heat and serve
